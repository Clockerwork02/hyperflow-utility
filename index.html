Token Burn Mechanisms

🔥
Fee Burns
A portion of protocol fees is permanently burned, reducing total supply and creating deflationary pressure to benefit all token holders.
  • 10% of vault performance fees burned
  • 5% of bridge fees burned
  • Automatic quarterly burn events
  • Transparent burn tracking
💸
Buyback Burns
Excess treasury funds are used to purchase FLOW tokens from the market and permanently burn them, directly supporting token price appreciation.
  • Monthly buyback programs
  • Market-supportive purchasing
  • Community-approved burn amounts
  • Price-positive market actions
