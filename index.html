FLOW Token Utility Framework

Core Utility Mechanisms

1. Governance & Voting Rights

FLOW token holders participate in protocol governance through on-chain voting mechanisms, proposal creation, and strategic decision-making processes that shape the future development of the ecosystem.

2. Revenue Sharing (70% Distribution)

Token holders receive 70% of all protocol-generated revenue through staking mechanisms, creating direct value accrual from platform success and sustainable reward distribution.

3. Yield Enhancement Multipliers

Staked FLOW tokens provide yield multipliers across all protocol strategies, with higher stakes unlocking premium yield opportunities and exclusive strategy access.

4. Fee Discounts & Priority Access

FLOW holders enjoy reduced transaction fees across all protocol interactions and priority access to new features, strategies, and partnership opportunities.

Supply Management
